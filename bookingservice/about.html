Our story & values

From one parking lot to an entire city.

ASP Car Care began as a weekend experiment in a small apartment parking lot in Hyderabad. Friends and neighbours wanted reliable, on-time washes without arguing about prices, waiting in queues, or worrying if the car was handled carefully.

What started as a 5–car routine quickly grew into a dedicated doorstep and self-drive wash service. Today, every booking that hits our system follows the same simple workflow: arrival window, checklist-driven wash, and a final SMS/WhatsApp update.

What we stand for.

  • 1
    Consistency over shortcuts. We follow a fixed wash sequence for every vehicle – from pre-rinse to interior vacuum – so you know exactly what you’re getting.
  • 2
    Transparent & fair pricing. Interior, exterior, teflon and combos are clearly listed in our quotation section, just like an Excel rate card – no surprises.
  • 3
    Respect for your time. Slots are planned so our team arrives prepared – especially for @Home washes where water and power access are coordinated in advance.
  • 4
    People-first team. Every wash partner is trained, verified and supported with checklists, SOPs and feedback – because happy teams create happy customers.
Journey so far
  • 2022 · Idea
    The weekend wash experiment.
    Started providing scheduled washes for a few apartments in Hyderabad to test if “on-time, hassle-free car care” could be productised.
  • 2023 · Launch
    ASP Car Care goes live.
    Introduced Self Drive, ASP Care and @Home services with OTP-based booking, digital receipts and a simple refund policy.
  • 2024 · Scale
    From one hub to multiple zones.
    Expanded coverage across key pockets of Hyderabad while fine-tuning slot management and live support chat for faster responses.
  • Next
    Memberships & multi-city presence.
    Rolling out membership plans, stronger partner network and eventually taking ASP Car Care beyond city limits.
