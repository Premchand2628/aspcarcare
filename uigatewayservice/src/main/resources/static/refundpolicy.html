Refund Policy

We aim to provide a smooth booking and service experience. This policy explains when you may be eligible for a refund, how refunds are calculated, and how to request one. By placing a booking through ASP Car Care, you agree to this Refund Policy.

Effective Date: 15-Dec-2025 Applies to: Self Drive · @Home · ASP Care Resolution-first: Re-service preferred

1) Overview

Refunds depend on service type, cancellation time, and whether the service has started. Platform/transaction fees (if any) may be non-refundable if charged by payment providers, unless required by law.

2) Eligibility for Refunds

Note: In most cases, we try to resolve issues via re-service or reschedule before refunds.

A) Full Refund (100%)

  • Service not delivered due to ASP Care cancellation (staff/operational issues).
  • Payment deducted but booking not confirmed due to technical issue.
  • Duplicate payment for the same booking.
  • @Home service not possible due to our team being unable to reach the location.

B) Partial Refund

  • Cancellation close to slot time (see cancellation window).
  • Service started but could not be completed due to customer-side reasons.
  • Downgrade/partial work requested (subject to assessment and approval).

C) No Refund

  • No-show or access not provided at the scheduled time.
  • Cancellation after service has started (unless a verified service-quality issue exists).
  • Service could not be performed due to customer-side constraints (incorrect address, vehicle unavailable, unsafe conditions, etc.).
  • @Home services where required water/power is not available on-site (if applicable to the chosen package).

3) Cancellation Window

Cancellation time is calculated from the slot start time (Self Drive) or the scheduled arrival window (@Home).

When you cancel Refund amount Notes
≥ 24 hours before slot 100% Full refund (excluding non-refundable gateway fees if applicable)
6–24 hours before slot 75% Operations planning cost may apply
1–6 hours before slot 50% Late cancellation
< 1 hour before slot 0% No refund (treated as last-minute/no-show)

4) Service Quality Issues

  • Report within 24 hours of completion (photos/videos help).
  • We may offer: free re-service (preferred), partial refund, or full refund for verified non-delivery/severe issues.

5) Refund Method & Timeline

  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method whenever possible.
  • Processing: Initiated within 1–3 business days.
  • Bank/payment provider settlement: typically 5–10 business days.

6) How to Request a Refund

  • In app: My Bookings → Booking Details → Help/Support → Raise Ticket
  • Chat Support: Tap Support and choose “Chat with Agent”
  • Email support: support@yourdomain.com (update this)
Please include: Booking ID, registered phone number, slot date/time, reason, and payment screenshot (if available).

7) Rescheduling (Optional)

  • Rescheduling is recommended instead of cancellation whenever possible.
  • Requests ≥ 6 hours before slot are usually free (subject to availability).
  • Requests within < 6 hours may be treated as a late cancellation.

8) Special Cases

  • Membership plans: Non-refundable once benefits are used or plan has started (unless required by law).
  • Coupons/offers: Refunds are calculated on the final paid amount (after discounts).
  • Cash payments: Refunds may be issued via UPI/bank transfer after verification.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted in the app/website with an updated effective date.
